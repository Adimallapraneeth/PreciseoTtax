Who Must File an FBAR?

U.S. citizens, U.S. residents, or entities (corporations, partnerships, LLCs)
Financial interest in or signature authority over foreign financial accounts
Aggregate value of all foreign accounts exceeds $10,000 at any time during the calendar year
